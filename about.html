Who are We?

Founded in 2025 by a group of passionate students in GROUP-G from Uganda Martyrs University, GROUP-G ELECTROMART was born from a simple idea of making technology accessible and affordable. What started as a university project has grown into a trusted online destination for gadgets.

To empower individuals and businesses by providing high-quality, innovative technology products with great customer service and expert guidance.

Customer-Centricity, Innovation, Integrity, Quality, Community Engagement.
