Who are We?

Founded in 2025 by a group of passionate students in GROUP-G from Uganda Martyrs University, GROUP-G ELECTROMART was born from a simple idea of make cutting-edge technology accessible and affordable for everyone. What started as a university project has grown into a trusted online destination for gadgets.
